Hi There, What’s Your Name?

Hi there, I am Yunbin! I am a M.S. student studying Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Maryland.

I have a strong research foundation in Humans and Society. It helps me understand users as human beings and bring out my genuine interest to understand people and empathize with their situations and problems when using technology.

During my undergrad years majoring in Sociology and US Media & Culture, I researched human interactions within digital media, including social networks and online communities, especially in international and cross-cultural contexts.

Working previously for a startup, I have learned the importance of over-communication with teams and ensuring research insights are actionable— able to effectively integrate into product design/development processes. As a UX enthusiast, I love how we bridge business goals with user experience.
